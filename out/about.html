About Us

Inspiring the Next Generation
of Creators

Smart Brain Creations is a Bangalore-based education initiative focused on integrating creative and digital learning into school systems.

Who We Are

We enable students to transition from learning to creating through structured, hands-on programs. Our programs are integrated directly within school environments, covering Design, Animation, AI, and Game Development.

India's AVGC (Animation, Visual Effects, Gaming & Comics) sector is part of the booming orange economy — the creative industries that are becoming a major driver of economic growth. We prepare students for careers in this fast-growing sector from an early age.

Our Purpose

Mission

To make every school a creative learning hub where students build real-world skills from an early stage.

Vision

A future where every student has access to creative technology education, regardless of their school's existing resources.

AVGC & Orange Economy

India's creative industries are projected to grow exponentially. We give students a head start in animation, gaming, digital design, and creative technology.

Fully Aligned with NEP 2020

FAQ

  • What is Smart Brain Creations?

    Smart Brain Creations is a creative education program that equips students with future-ready digital, design, and innovation skills.

  • Is it aligned with NEP 2020?

    Yes, Smart Brain Creations supports NEP 2020 by promoting skill-based, experiential, and multidisciplinary learning.

  • What do students gain?

    Students gain creativity, digital literacy, problem-solving abilities, and hands-on industry-relevant skills.

  • Do schools need heavy investment?

    No, Smart Brain Creations provides the required curriculum, faculty, and lab support with minimal investment from schools.

  • Is a demo available?

    Yes, we offer free demo sessions for schools to experience the program before implementation.
